Get to know me

“Hi there! My name is Jael and I love being creative! My hobbies include dancing, reading and baking! Designing jackets is another one of my hobbies I enjoy. I’ve always had a passion for expressing creativity through so many ways. I started designing jean jackets last year and I’ve loved it much more ever since. It’s fun working on projects and making ideas come to life. When I’m not designing jackets, I spend most of my time volunteering and teaching others. I also love and enjoy catching up with friends. DBJ has only been possible with support, so thank you for supporting my little business and making it possible to share what I love. Feel free to contact me with any questions through my social media regarding orders, designs & or policies! I’d love to chat with you!“
